Downtown Issue

OVERVIEW

(2022)
This special "Downtown" issue highlighted 50 influential New Yorkers in the arts and culture space, living and working in Lower Manhattan.

The goal was to create a snap grid effect, similar to The New York Times' Best Actors of 2021 issue, using minimal JavaScript and additional libraries. I served as the Front-End Lead on this project and collaborated with the design team to develop ideas for the page design.
