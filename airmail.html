AIR MAIL is a digital media startup, launched by former Vanity Fair editor-in-chief Graydon Carter.

During my time at Air Mail, I had the opportunity to contribute to and lead several impactful projects. Below are three of my favorites.

Downtown Issue

OVERVIEW

(2022)
This special "Downtown" issue highlighted 50 influential New Yorkers in the arts and culture space, living and working in Lower Manhattan.

The goal was to find a simple way to create a snap grid effect, similar to the animation used in The New York Times' Best Actors of 2021 issue. I served as the Front-End Lead on this project and collaborated with the design team to develop ideas for the page design.

Online Shop

OVERVIEW

(2021 - 2023)
The shop started as a simple Shopify theme, initially selling only AIR MAIL-branded products. The goal was to move to Shopify to establish an online storefront and eliminate the manual order fulfillment process. Since then, the shop has expanded to sell not only AIR MAIL products but also dropship and affiliate items from brands across the U.S., Canada, and the U.K.

Today, the shop is a fully headless theme, utilizing Shopify as the backend for the checkout process. My role as the Lead Shopify Developer involved overseeing all front-end development, design, and conversion rate optimization for the shop.

Arts and Culture

OVERVIEW

(2024)
Arts Intel, AIR MAIL's culture vertical, serves as a comprehensive guide for cultural happenings worldwide. It offers up-to-date information on gallery openings, movie screenings, exhibitions, top restaurants and hotels, as well as TV shows and podcasts, spanning cities from London and Los Angeles to New York and Milan.

I collaborated with another front-end developer, back-end developers, and the design team to redesign the City and Events pages, improving user experience, visual engagement, and navigation.
