Online Shop

OVERVIEW

(2021 - 2023)
The shop started as a simple Shopify theme, initially selling only AIR MAIL-branded products. The goal was to move to Shopify to establish a storefront and eliminate the manual order fulfillment process. Since then, the shop has expanded to sell not only AIR MAIL products but also dropship and affiliate items from brands across the U.S., Canada, and the U.K.

Today, the shop is a fully headless theme, utilizing Shopify as the backend for the checkout process. My role as a Shopify Developer involved overseeing all front-end development, design, and conversion rate optimization for the shop.
