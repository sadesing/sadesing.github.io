A little about me

I currently work as a Front-End Software Developer in the e-commerce space. Alumna of University at Buffalo and Texas Southern University. My love for all things code began in my early teens, during the Myspace era, customizing layouts for myself and friends. I enjoy bridging the gap between design and engineering, combining my technical knowledge and keen eye for design to create meaningful and inclusive products.

Aside from coding, I enjoy empowering underrepresented groups in STEM, traveling, spending time with loved ones, discovering new cuisines and photography. I also serve on the Leadership Board as the Community Outreach Lead for the Black Girls CODE Dallas Chapter and as Webmaster for the Engineers Without Borders North Texas Chapter. Previously, I was an Evangelist/Advocate for Women Who Code DFW.

I seek to be inspired, to envision the unlikely, to work hard for things that are worth it, and to be surrounded by those who bring out the best in me.
