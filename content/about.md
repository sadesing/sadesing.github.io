Hi, I’m Sade. I’m a software engineer and a graduate student in cybersecurity and data privacy at Albany Law School.

My background in systems engineering and human factors shapes how I approach problems. I’m most interested in how people interact with technology, and how these interactions surface privacy, security, and compliance challenges. I enjoy identifying gaps and coming up with solutions that are both practical and human-centered.

I started this blog to document what I’m learning, share projects I’m working on, and contribute to conversations around ethical and responsible tech.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling, photography, exploring new cuisines, and spending time with family. I also look for ways to give back through community work.
