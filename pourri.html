Overview of Pourri Project

Pourri also known as Poo~Pourri

Shopify Plus U.S.A. & International Stores

OVERVIEW

(2020 - 2021)
At Poo~Pourri, I joined the direct-to-consumer (D2C) team as a front-end developer to support the company's expansion into international e-commerce. I helped launch and maintain Shopify Plus storefronts across multiple regions while contributing to new features for the main U.S. store.

I led the setup and front-end customization of the Kuwait and Canada stores, ensuring brand consistency and smooth localization. On the U.S. store, I helped develop product page enhancements, including a Build Your Own Bundle feature, that allowed customers to easily group related products.

This project sharpened my skills in Shopify theme development, localization, and multi-region support. I worked closely with designers and senior developers to push updates that improved both the site experience and overall functionality.
