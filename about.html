I currently work as a Front-End Software Engineer at AIR MAIL. Alumna of University at Buffalo and Texas Southern University. My love for all things code began in my early teens, during the Myspace era, customizing layouts for myself and friends. I enjoy bridging the gap between design and engineering, combining my technical knowledge and keen eye for design to create meaningful products.

Aside from coding, I enjoy empowering underrepresented groups in STEM, traveling, spending time with loved ones, and photography. I also serve on the Leadership Board as the Community Outreach Lead for the Black Girls CODE Dallas Chapter and as Webmaster for the Engineers Without Borders North Texas Chapter. Previously, I was an Evangelist/Advocate for Women Who Code DFW.
